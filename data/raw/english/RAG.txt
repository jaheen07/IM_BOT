## 1. COMPANY PROFILE


### 1.1 About Acme AI Ltd.


Acme AI Ltd. is a forward-thinking technology startup established in 2020 by Syed Sharek Ahmed Roomy, with Syed Sadhli Ahmed Roomy as Co-Founder. The company specializes in providing high-quality data annotation services that enhance artificial intelligence and machine learning systems. In addition to annotation services, Acme AI operates a dedicated Machine Learning team, delivers offshore support services, and conducts professional training and certification programs related to data annotation and AI. The organization is committed to innovation, operational excellence, and long-term growth in the AI ecosystem. Furthermore, Acme AI Ltd. is a subsidiary company of Acme Technologies. The parent company provides strategic oversight and governance guidance; however, Acme AI Ltd. operates as a separate legal entity with its own management, employees, and internal policies, unless otherwise specified.


### 1.2 Company Location


Acme AI Ltd. is located at House 385, Road 06, Avenue 03, Mirpur DOHS, Dhaka-1216.


### 1.3 Company Type


Acme AI Ltd. is a limited liability company (Ltd.), meaning it is a legally distinct entity from its shareholders. The liability of shareholders is limited to the amount of their investment in the company. This structure provides legal stability, operational continuity, and enhanced governance for employees and stakeholders.


### 1.4 Vision
The vision of Acme AI Ltd. is to become the leading annotation service provider while diversifying into the development of proprietary AI-driven products that contribute to global technological advancement.


### 1.5 Mission


The mission of Acme AI Ltd. is to deliver accurate, scalable, and reliable AI data solutions, foster a culture of discipline and continuous improvement, support employee growth, and maintain strong, ethical partnerships with clients worldwide.


### 1.6 Core Values


Acme AI Ltd. operates based on core values that include integrity, quality, accountability, teamwork, discipline, and innovation. These values guide all decisions, behaviors, and interactions within the organization.

## 2. ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE


### 2.1 Governance and Leadership


The governance of Acme AI Ltd. is led by the Chairman, Major General Syed Fatemy Ahmed Roomy (Retd.), supported by the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Co-Founder, Sadhli Ahmed Roomy. The company operates under the guidance of eight directors who provide strategic oversight and policy direction.


### 2.2 Management Hierarchy


The organizational hierarchy follows a structured chain of command beginning with the Chairman and CEO, followed by the Operation Lead and Operation Manager. Operational execution is managed by Project Coordinators, who supervise Team Leads and Assistant Team Leads. Production Officers, Annotators, and support staff operate under this framework to ensure accountability, quality, and efficiency.


### 2.3 Departments


Acme AI Ltd. is organized into several functional departments including Operations, Human Resources (People), Finance, Strategic Support, Machine Learning, Information Technology, and Administration. Each department has several defined roles and responsibilities aligned with organizational objectives.


## 3. PRODUCTS AND SERVICES


### 3.1 Core Services


Acme AI Ltd. provides comprehensive data annotation services across image, video, and text-based datasets. The company also supports Large Language Model (LLM), Local and International AI projects and delivers offshore operational services to international clients.


### 3.2 Training and Certification


In addition to service delivery, Acme AI Ltd. offers professional training programs, including the Image Labeling Associate Certificate, aimed at developing skilled annotation professionals and strengthening industry capacity.


## 4. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES


### 4.1 Employment Types


The company engages employees under several categories including full-time employees, contractual or freelance personnel, and interns. Employment terms vary based on role, duration, and operational requirements.



### 4.2 Remote and Home Office Policy


Employees may request to work from home by formally emailing their immediate supervisor and the HR department before the start of office hours. Approval is subject to operational feasibility and managerial discretion. At the end of each quarter, if Acme AI meets the minimum required profit set by the authority, 15% of the profit will be shared with employees who have served for at least a year, calculated on a prorated basis. It is also known as quarterly bonus.


## 5. ATTENDANCE AND DISCIPLINE


Attendance at Acme AI Ltd. is monitored through electronic fingerprint devices and the ERP system. Employees are required to record attendance at the start and end of their workday. A grace period of five minutes is allowed, after which late entry is recorded. Repeated or habitual lateness may result in salary deductions and disciplinary actions as per company policy.


To encourage punctuality, the company offers an attendance incentive. Employees who maintain one hundred percent attendance and arrive on time are eligible for a monthly attendance bonus of BDT 1,000. Remote employees may also qualify for this incentive by completing additional assigned work.

## 6. COMPENSATION AND SALARY STRUCTURE


Employee compensation at Acme AI Ltd. is structured into multiple components. The basic salary constitutes 31.25 percent of the gross salary, with allowances such as, house rent, medical, transport, mobile, and internet allowances calculated as defined percentages of the basic salary. Below is the official salary structure for each employee based on company policy: 


Basic Salary: 31.25% of Gross Salary.
House Rent Allowance: 100% of Basic Salary. 
Medical Allowance: 60% of Basic Salary. 
Transport Allowance: 35% of Basic Salary. 
Mobile Allowance: 15% of Basic Salary.


The salary month runs from the 26th of one month to the 25th of the following month. Salaries are disbursed on the first working day of each calendar month. Annual salary increments are determined through performance evaluations conducted at the end of the fiscal year, subject to management approval. In cases of position changes or other occasions where an increment is warranted, any adjustments are solely determined by the judgment and decision of higher authorities within the company. 


### 6.1 Bonus


Permanent employees who have completed 6 months of service are eligible for 50% of their total gross salary as the Eid Bonus. 


Employees who have not yet completed 6 months will receive a prorated bonus. The bonus amount is calculated as 25% of their gross salary, multiplied by the number of completed months of service, and then divided by the probation period in months.

### 8.2 Casual Leave


Employees at Acme AI are entitled to 10 Casual leaves. A maximum of one (01) day of casual leave can be taken at once. The minimum notice period required for applying for casual leave is 2 days in advance for approval. 


### 8.3 Sick Leave


Employees at Acme AI are entitled to 14 Sick leaves. They can take the sick leave at any time subject to providing evidence of sickness e.g., diagnosis and prescription. Any duration beyond one day requires a valid doctor’s certificate or medical document. Sick leave must be reported as early as possible and before office hours commence. 


### 8.4 Bridge Leave


Bridge leave at Acme AI Ltd. refers to taking leave that spans across a weekly holiday, typically Saturday. For example, if you take leave on Friday and the following Sunday, this is considered bridge leave. In this scenario, it will count as 3 days of leave taken.


### 8.5 Earned/Annual Leave


Effective from January 1, employees are entitled to 16 annual leave days after one year of service, distributed evenly across four quarters. Leave can be encashed up to 4 days per quarter, with any unused leave converted to salary at quarter-end.


### 8.6 Earned Leave


Encashment amounts are based on daily wages at the time of encashment. Rules for taking annual leave are outlined in our HR Policy. The policy undergoes periodic review. Please review the full policy for compliance details.


### 8.7 Adjustment leave


At Acme AI Ltd., adjustment leave is granted when an employee works on a weekend or off-day. This leave allows employees to balance their work commitments by compensating them with time off for their additional efforts on non-standard workdays. 


### 8.8 Maternity leave


At Acme AI Ltd., our maternity leave policy requires employees to have worked for at least 6 months and have confirmed job status. Maternity leave spans 120 days, providing adequate time for maternity-related needs. This leave is only for company's female employee. Any male employee is not eligible for this leave.


### 8.9 Leave Approval


Leave approval at Acme AI Ltd. depends on the current workload, work balance, and the supervisor's planning. While we strive to accommodate leave requests for the benefit of our employees' well-being and work-life balance, approval is not guaranteed and is subject to the company's operational needs. This ensures that both individual needs and business requirements are balanced effectively.
